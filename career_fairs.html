Third Annual John D. Thompson Entrepreneurial Development Seminar:


Unleash the entrepreneur within the engineer!

January 28, 2016, 4:00 PM, McGill Faculty Club (3450 McTavish)

Keynote address given by Luc Sirois of Hacking Health, excellent panelists and audience discussions on how to make a difference through entrepreneurial endeavours, followed by a networking cocktail. This seminar is part of the McGill EUS TechWeek (January 25-29), a week-long event focused on celebrating and showcasing the engineering profession, as well as providing students with an opportunity to learn more about various career opportunities in engineering. - Full details and required registration at: http://www.mcgill.ca/engineering/research/ice/john-d-thompson-entrepreneurial-development-seminar



Doing business with Anges Quebec (in English) – February 10


RegistrationMcGill TechFair – Thursday, February 4, 2016

What is an angel investor? Join us for this webinar that will demystify the topic. In this hour-long presentation, we will cover the following topics: what is Anges Québec, what are the members’ expectations and objectives, what is the investment process and what criteria should a business fulfill to maximize the chances of obtaining an investment.
