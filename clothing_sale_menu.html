Clothing Sale Menu

How to Order

Below are the options you can choose from for the clothing sale order. To make an order, you must go in person to the CS lounge (Trottier 1060) between 10:30AM and 2:30PM on Monday Feb 22nd or Tuesday Feb 23rd with the correct total amount in either cash or cheque. Ask for the CSUS representative on duty to make your order and pay.

About the Apparel

All options come with the McGill crest and "McGill School of Computer Science" embroidered onto the left chest area, except for the T-Shirt which will carry the same design in screen print on the centre. For the Hooded Zip-Up Sweatshirts you have the option of requesting your program to be printed on the back for an additional $5.00.

Samples are available in the CS lounge (Trottier 1060) for all items in various sizes. If you are not sure of the material or the size, please ask the CSUS representative on duty for the samples for the items you want to order.
