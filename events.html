CS Summer Camp needs Stream Leaders and Volunteers



Wanna inspire the next generation of computer scientists and programmers? Enjoy working with teens or are passionate about teaching CS? If so come on down and help run McGill’s CS Summer Camp.

McGill’s CS Summer Camp takes students ages 15-17 and teaches them what it's like to be a computer scientist. They enroll in 3 streams: game design, robotics, or bioinformatics/medical computing.

We are currently looking for 3-4 people to teach students and lead volunteers in either robotics, game design, or bioinformatics. Celebrate at the end with your fellow volunteers and the campers families’ as your campers compete in a friendly programming competition with a BBQ at the end! This is a real opportunity to do some good for the community and impact some lives. If you are interested please contact: Joseph Vybinhal at jvybihal@cs.mcgill.ca
