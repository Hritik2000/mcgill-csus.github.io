Comp Sci & Biology Rep

Kevin is a 3rd year student in the Joint Computer Science and Biology program. Historically, CS/Bio students did not have any form of student government or representation. Not quite fitting in with MSBU, CS/Bio student often tend to participate more in the CS community. Kevin wishes to help provide direction and build a community to the CS/Bio students. Direction in the form of helping shape the academic curriculum to student’s needs and providing post-graduate and research opportunities and advice. Since this is a small program of less than 70 students, by holding specific social events for CS/Bio events CS/Bio student can meet each other to help build a community.

As a person, Kevin is aight. Kevin is interested in health technologies, medicine, business, and video games, or any mix of these topics. Feel free to contact me any time on Facebook or email: kevin.luk@mail.mcgill.ca
